Dentures in One Day
One of the very few Dental clinics in Mumbai to provide this service, our in- house lab and amazing technicians make sure that you get your perfect dentures within 24 hours of your visit to the clinic. Leaving you with no discomfort,that going through days and, at times, even weeks, may cause you.

Depending upon the number of decayed or missing teeth in your mouth, you may need a complete denture or partial denture.

Types of Dentures we provide:
-Complete dentures:
Complete dentures replace all of a patientâ€™s teeth. They sit on top of the gums, as opposed to dental bridges that are anchored to existing teeth. We deliver complete dentures in one day

-Removable partial dentures:
Partial dentures are used when a patient still has some of his or her natural teeth, such as when one or more teeth remain in the upper and lower jaw.They are convenient and removable, which means you can take them out whenever you need to. They help to prevent the other teeth from moving and are an alternative to dental bridges and dental implants

-Cast partial dentures:
A Cast Partial Denture (CPD) is a removable partial denture consisting of a cast metal framework that contains artificial teeth set in an acrylic resin. They are used when some of the natural teeth are remaining. They help to prevent the other teeth from moving and are an alternative to dental bridges and dental implants

-Implant supported dentures:
They are also called as snap-in dentures. These dentures are the most effective choice when it comes to stability. They are held securely in place with the help of dental implants.

Benefits of Dentures:

1. Replaces missing teeth, restores normal function, restores normal bite in case of collapsed bite.
2. Prevents over eruption of opposing teeth, prevents drift/ tipping of adjacent teeth.
3. Prevents bone loss thus preventing periodontal disease.
4. A face without teeth can sag and appear sunken. Dentures allow you to maintain the natural shape of your face and smile.
5. You can bite naturally, eat virtually anything you want and, experience the full taste of the food you eat with Dentures.
6. It is an economical option for replacement of teeth.
